Marvel Movies I enjoy

Sometimes to do what is right we have to give up the things we want the most even our dreams.

Spidermans latest movie NO WAY HOME in my opinion really shattered me into bits. After seeing what this Peter Parker (Tom Holland) has gone through over these passed years this movie was a rewatch. I enjoy this Spiderman a lot more only because how many times he's been torn turns out he did want revenge but other universes helped him out to go down that path.

Doctor Strange when I first heard of him was really fully of himself; well this time around in this movie he showed the changes people can make and the choices you make in life.

I enjoy Iron Man for this stubborness and how cocky he can be. Through-out the Marvel movies with the Avengers he's proven different personas of himself depending on his surroundings. In the latest movie he's proven to have a heart not just for his loved ones but the universe.
